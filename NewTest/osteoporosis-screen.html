Know Your Bone Health Risk

Osteoporosis — the thinning of bones — affects 1 in 2 women over 50 and is a major cause of fractures. Most people have no symptoms until a break occurs. This screen helps you identify risk factors so you can take action before a fracture happens.

5 Has a parent or sibling been diagnosed with osteoporosis or had a hip fracture?
